Bringing Algerian professionals in North America together to build meaningful connections and drive growth between communities.

We are a team of committed professionals – engineers, entrepreneurs, researchers – united to build the North American Algerian Alliance (NAAA). Our mission: federate Algerian excellence across the continent, foster mentorship and innovation, and create lasting bridges with our mother country, Algeria.

Our strategic pillars

What we do

Connection & network
Facilitating access to hidden opportunities in the North American market. Breaking the isolation of skilled newcomers and building a vibrant network of Algerian professionals.
Development & Mentorship
Mentorship programs (Seniors to Juniors). Skill-building workshops: Leadership, Negotiation, Tech — to accelerate careers.
Investment & Impact
Channel diaspora investment toward Algeria. Provide technical expertise for development projects and build sustainable economic bridges.

Investment Criteria

What we look for

Excellence

Bringing together Algerian talent across North America to build a community recognized for its expertise, professionalism, and high standards.

Mentorship mindset

Sharing knowledge and experience to accelerate careers, through structured mentorship between senior professionals and the next generation.

Giving back

Channeling our skills and resources toward impactful projects in Algeria, turning diaspora success into tangible contributions.

Ambition & impact

Turning individual achievements into collective power, to shape economic and technological exchanges between Algeria and North America.

The three profiles

Ambitious students, established professionals, and visionary benefactors — each playing a vital role in our ecosystem.

Bridge builders

Creating lasting bridges between Algeria and North America by connecting talent, opportunities, and expertise across borders.

Founding partners

Join the early builders of the alliance and help lay the foundation for a premier Algerian network across North America.
